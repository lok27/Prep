How to make a Grilled Cheese

Whether if it's an afterschool snack or a drunk snack, a grilled cheese sandwich is always a sure way to satisfy a rumbling stomach. In this recipe, we will show you how to make the most satisfying grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of choice
  • 200 grams of butter
  • 200 grams of cream cheese of choice
  • 1 slice of American Cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice of Asiago cheese

Direction

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Make sure to spread evenly and cover all sides and corners. Do the same with the cream cheese to the other side of each slice.
  2. Heat up a dry frying pan and put on the both slices of bread, butter side down. Turn down the heat to medium once the bread is in. Heat control is important, make sure not to burn the bread.
  3. Put one slice of cheese on each piece of bread. Let it sit for 1 minute. Then flip one side on top of the other.
  4. Toast each side of the sandwich until golden brown. Cut in half and serve!
